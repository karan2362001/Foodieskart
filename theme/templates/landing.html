Hotel Management
Software.

Our hotel management software offers a comprehensive solution to streamline various aspects of hotel operations, ensuring efficiency, convenience, and exceptional guest experiences. Let's delve into the details of each module: Tailwind CSS and based on the components from the Flowbite Library and the Blocks System.

1: Supply Chain of Food

Efficiently manage the supply chain of food items, from procurement to storage, ensuring freshness and quality. Track inventory levels, monitor supplier relationships, and optimize procurement processes to minimize costs while maximizing quality.

  • Categorization of food items (vegetables, fruits, spices, dairy products, etc.).
  • Optimize food procurement and inventory management processes.

2: Table Ordering using QR Code

Revolutionize the dining experience with our QR code-based table ordering system. Guests can simply scan the QR code at their table to access the menu, place orders, and make payments seamlessly. Enhance efficiency, reduce wait times, and empower guests with greater control over their dining experience.

  • Transform the dining experience with seamless table-side ordering.
  • auto generated bill of one table(by qr order , captain app)

3: Online Integration

Integrate with popular online marketplaces such as Zomato and Swiggy to expand your reach and attract more customers. Manage online orders seamlessly within the same system, ensuring consistency in service quality and order fulfilment.

  • Expand your reach and attract more customers by integrating with popular online food delivery platforms.
  • integration zometo,swiggy etc..

4: Payment Dashboard

Centralize payment processing with our intuitive payment dashboard. Monitor transactions in real-time, track revenue streams, and analyse payment trends effortlessly. Provide guests with multiple payment options for enhanced convenience and flexibility

  • Centralize payment processing and monitor transactions in real-time.
  • 1: payment of the day,month,year
  • 2: market place payment details
  • 3: dine in payment details
  • 4: take away payment details
  • 5: cash and online mode filter

5: Billing Solutions

Simplify billing processes with our comprehensive billing solutions. Generate accurate invoices, automate billing cycles, and manage accounts receivable efficiently. Ensure transparency and accuracy in financial transactions to foster trust and satisfaction among guests.

  • Simplify billing processes and ensure accuracy in financial transactions.
  • auto generated bill of one table(by qr order , captain app)

6: Receptionist Dashboard

Empower receptionists with a user-friendly dashboard to manage guest bookings, inquiries, and requests seamlessly. Streamline check-in/check-out processes, handle reservations efficiently, and provide personalized assistance to enhance the overall guest experience.

  • Simplify billing processes and ensure accuracy in financial transactions.
  • Handling table availability and bookings.
  • Responding to customer queries and requests.

7: Staff Management

Efficiently manage your workforce with our staff management module. Streamline attendance tracking, automate scheduling, and handle payroll processing with ease. Empower your staff with the tools they need to deliver exceptional service while optimizing operational efficiency.

  • Streamline workforce management with features for attendance tracking, scheduling, and payroll processing.

8: Inventory Management

Optimize inventory management with our advanced inventory tracking system. Monitor stock levels in real-time, automate replenishment processes, and minimize wastage to maximize profitability. Ensure availability of essential supplies while minimizing excess inventory holding costs.

  • Optimize inventory tracking and minimize wastage to maximize profitability.
